## ⚙️ Settings & Setup

- **Interface**
  - Disable *Auto Erase Map Marks* → keeps your layout lines.
  - Disable *Factory Guard* → constructors follow factory orders instead of guarding.

- **Performance**
  - Raptors mod needs a strong PC. On weak PCs, set GFX to *Lowest* (better FPS and visibility).
  - Example setup: Ryzen 5900X, 64 GB DDR4, Radeon 5700XT. Playing at 2k res with 144hz and vsync enabled, I have "Graphics Quality" on lowest.

## 👀 For New Players
- **Spec a few games** before jumping in to learn how Raptors flow.
- **Corners vs. Sides:**
  - **Corner** = fewer defense lines, more eco space, but attracts raptors to side if eco is too high.
                 Fast's dislikes playing next to a high eco corner cause of the additional aggro.
  - **Side** = critical wall between teammates, requires stronger defenses.

- Backline are for EFUS, don't overbuild or you chain you'r neighbour.

## ⚡ Economy & Efficiency
- **T1 Converters**
  - Cheap (1M, T2 cost 380M), but inefficient and space-heavy.
  - Only use up to ~20–30k Energy.

- **T2/T3 Power**
  - **T2 Converters** + **T2 Wind** → bridge to EFUS.
  - **Wind (25 Wind map example)** = ~2× more efficient than Fusions.
  - **EFUS**: Place far from your base (eco attracts Raptors).

- **Shields**
  - **T3 Shield** have a better efficiency than T2.
  - Core Sanctuary = strong, but explodes like a nuke.
  - ARM shields chain if too close together.
  - Legion shield is the best choice.

- **Other Notes**
  - 3× T1 Turrets = same build power as 1× T2 (but cheaper).
  - ARM Com gets EXP from building and shares it with future builds → . Buildings that have maxed EXP are OP.

## 🏰 Defenses
- **Walls**
  - T1 = paper.
  - T2 = weak.
  - Quad Guard = crush-safe.

- **Fire Weapons** (Raptors hate fire 🔥)
  - Core Dragon’s Maw / Legion Dragon's Maw = excellent. But they burn eggs (thanks to Frizt for the info).

- **Epic Defenses**
  - Legion Bastion = strong but does friendly fire.
  - ARM com Pulsar > Bulwark > Pulsar
  - Olympus (short range) < Basilica (mid) < Basilisk (long).
  - All artillery weapons are bad (low DPS vs cost, lots of friendly fire).
  - **Rule:** Close EPIC walls (`Ctrl+X → On`) for them to block.

- **AA (Anti-Air)**
  - End of grace: 3+ Flak or plenty of T1 AA.

- **Special case**
  - Juno is required at 50% EVO about 8-12 in front of you’r entrance, Fast does them as a line on repeat. Juno kills Kamikaze and Paralyzer - Kamikaze insta kills your defences when it reaches. Keep in mind that Juno is a little bomb.

## 📈 Build Orders & Starts

### Fast’s Start
1. ~30× T1 Wind
2. 10× T1 Turrets → upgrade path to T2 Wind
3. 8× T1 Turrets for T1 Converters
4. Add more T1 Turrets only when **Metal balance is positive**
5. Goal:
   - 20–30k Energy with T1 Converters
   - 100k Energy from T2 Wind + Converters before first EFUS
6. E Store about 1.5 times of your income is important!
7. 16+ Dragon’s Maw minimum at least 1min before grace

**Golden Rules:**
- Only build turrets when Metal income is positive.
- Never build Converters next to Fusions (boom chains). Leave **2–3 rows of turrets** between.
- Split Converters into **separate areas** (left, middle, middle, right).

## 🧠 Advanced Tactics
- **Reclaim Farming**
  - Use ARM Beamer / Core Twin Guard + drones on repeat reclaim.
  - Hives = strong AA, farm carefully.
  - Fewer farmers = more metal per hive (don’t stack).
  - Farming is recommened only for high apm / advanced people.

- **Builder Tricks**
  - Any Con can build other factions’ T2 at +70% cost.
  - Any Con (including Com) can skip queue with **N**.
  - ALT+SHIFT+SPACE = split builders.
  - Base Builder & T3 Air Aide = can resurrect.
  - AIR transports can carry Base Builder → drop by self-destruct/reclaim.

- **Combat Tips**
  - SPACE+X = shows bomb radius of buildings.
  - All Coms can set **DGun to repeat**.
    - Core Com → repeat line DGun.
    - Legion Com → shotgun spread.
    - ARM Com -> Sniper
  - Frontline T3 Turrets in repeat mode → reclaim fields automatically.

## 🚫 Common Mistakes

- Building too far forward → steals aggro from teammate.
- Not building on frontline → neighbor dies.
- Converters too close → chain-explosions with Fusions.
- Ignoring shields → unshielded units/buildings die fast.

---

## 👑 Queens & Endgame
- Before Queens arrive:
  - 2× layers of closed EPIC walls - put them "off" just after build as you eager queens else. Also closed EPIC's have 4x resist/health.
  - Have backline AA and shields for all your eco.
  - Side walls planned (fort setup).
- Killing Penguins early → you can delay eco shields until ~5 minutes before Queens.
- Queens don’t explode on reclaim → but takes extreme luck and buildpower to time right.
- T3 Launcher (Cortex Gantry → Commando) → only shoot half at your front, keep rest for emergencies.
