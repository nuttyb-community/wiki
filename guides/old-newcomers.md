# Old NuttyB Raptor Guide by Cynder Quinn (CQs)

## Cortex NuttyB Raptor Guide

The aim of this guide is to give you a basic understanding of what level of economy you can get away with at the start of a NuttyB raptor game with a 10 minute grace period. There will always be more “optimal” ways to open for more raptor agro but this guide is for beginners who shouldn’t be worrying about agro and more about surviving.
The image below is what the build will be based on using the 11x11 square shape as its design. The build can however be used on larger bases with a little adaptation.
Each “square” is the size of the numbers on the edges of the base which is 4x4 on the build grid.
Notes are marked in red for tips and additional comments.


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_010.webp)


Step 1 is to pick your preferred lab. Either using the bot lab or vehicle lab is fine for this build. Using the air lab is movement efficient but lacks build power. **NOTE: Bot lab is the cheapest but slow movement speed. Vehicle lab is more expensive but has a bit more build power and better movement speed**
When the game starts or ideally in the short time before the game starts you want to queue up your chosen lab and your first grid of 16 T1 wind generators with your commander. Then when the game starts select your first air drone above your commander to make another three grids of T1 wind generators (48) Note: Allow for roughly 24 wind to be built before moving on to step 3 and making T2 Lab

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_025.webp)


While the T1 wind is being constructed, queue up 3 constructors from your chosen lab. Once they are done send them to construct 4 T1 constructor turrets in the corners of a grid above your T1 wind being built. When those are built, start on your T2 lab and queue up T2 constructors.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_039.webp)


While you’re constructing your T2 constructors set your base builder turret to reclaim your T1 lab this will give you some metal back. Once you’ve made your 5 T2 constructors you can also reclaim your T2 lab.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_049.webp)


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_050.webp)


Once 3-6 converters are completed and 6 or more T2 wind generators are complete send one of your constructors off the T2 wind production to make 2 T2 construction turrets above your T2 wind production
**NOTE: 16 T2 wind generators gives you 4000E 6 T2 converts use 3600E. Use this as a ratio guide when building. Micro your con turrets to things you need more of.**

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_062.webp)


Now you want to take the constructor from the T2 construction turrets and another from your T2 wind production and send them to the front door of your base line. These will then be used to make defences.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_091.webp)


Now gather up your T1 constructors as well as your drones which by now you’ll have 4-5 of flying around your commander and send them all to build your side walls.
**NOTE: Using Shift + Left mouse click dragging + spacebar will divide up the tasks evenly between your constructors making for more efficient and speedy building.**

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_125.webp)


It’s time to start on our front line defenses. First we make 4 T2 construction turrets at the front behind where our walls will be if not already there.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_143.webp)


Next we split our two constructors into two tasks. One starts making 4 AA turrets just behind our construction turrets. The other makes 10 Quad guard towers just behind our walls.
**NOTE: Quad guards are used because they are cheap on metal for good DPS**

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_156.webp)


Now we use our Commander to make 10 dragon’s maw flame turrets in front of our walls and Quad guards. You can also add 4 extra dragon’s maws out in front of those to absorb some kamikaze raptors.

**NOTE: Kamikaze raptors are killed by juno’s attack electric field**

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_161.webp)


Final step for the basic defences is to build 2 Shield generators either side of your construction turrets covering almost all of your base wall size. These will absorb any early artillery shots. After the shields are done or while they’re building you can queue your constructors to start making T2 walls at the front of your base for added cheap defence. Raptors will be spawning at this point so bear that in mind and use your commander to apply extra DPS/protection where needed.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_176.webp)


You will now also be wanting to start production on Juno’s which can be used to destroy/stop kamikaze raptors from getting close to your base. You’ll want to place these along the side walls of your base ideally at the back or middle section. 3 is a good number to start with but you’ll want 6+ going forward

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_180.webp)


Now it’s time to expand your ECONOMY mirror the eco that you’ve already made but not the T1 wind generators. This is around the time you can move into AFUS production as well depending on how much agro you’ve got.
**NOTE: Going straight for AFUS will provide more E but will be slower to build. Also note how many converters are being built there is more than your E production this allows for you to build your first AFUS after your T2 wind is done and it gives you value as soon as it’s done since you’re only on 5 Constructors this is efficient build time in the long run.**

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_192.webp)


You will want to add extra Quad guards to the sides along with con turrets and shields at this time to prevent your sides getting sneaky raptors or unlucky arty shots
**NOTE: The extra side defences might not be needed. You might wish to just fill out the line of con turrets and shields and out repair any raptors that come at the sides.**

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_217.webp)


Now your base should look like the image below and you should be thinking about adding the Advanced Fusion reactors as seen in the image. Do this only if your front line defences are holding and you’ve got the game under control. More E production means more raptor aggression so don’t go rushing to get fusions if you’re not ready for it.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_222.webp)


Now you have a stable ECO and are looking to increase it. You also need to consider protecting it as we go further into the game. Getting extra back line shields will prevent your ECO being destroyed by artillery raptors.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_238.webp)


You’ve reached the end of this basic guide with this build you should be able to survive the first wave of raptors even at 7x wave multiplier if done correctly. I recommend practicing the build order in a solo game giving yourself a 10 min timer. 
## Armada NuttyB Raptor Guide

The aim of this guide is to give you a basic understanding of what level of economy you can get away with at the start of a NuttyB raptor game with a 10 minute grace period. There will always be more “optimal” ways to open for more raptor agro but this guide is for beginners who shouldn’t be worrying about agro and more about surviving.
The image below is what the build will be based on using the 16x12 square shape as its design. The build can however be used on larger bases with a little adaptation.
Each “square” is the size of the numbers on the edges of the base which is 4x4 on the build grid.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_271.webp)

**PROS:**
ARMADA HAS THE CHEAPEST T2 WIND
THE STRONGEST DEFENCES TO HOLD UNTIL MID-GAME
LONG RANGE COMMANDER TO SNIPE EARLY TOUGH RAPTORS
TRANSFERS COMMANDERS EXP TO DEFENCES BUILT WITH THE COMMANDER
HIGHER ENERGY COSTS FOR DEFENCES LESS METAL COST
LONGEST RANGE T2 DEFENCES
**CONS:**
MOST PUNISHING IF YOU MESS UP YOUR BUILD
COMMANDER IS WEAKER TO START (LESS HP LESS DPS)
CAN BE TRICKY TO MANAGE EARLY METAL INCOME
SLOW FIRE RATE DEFENCES CAN LEAD TO BEING OVERRUN

**TLDR:**
START 64 T1 WINDS - 8 T1 CON TURRETS IN MIDDLE - T2 LAB - 3 T2 CONVERTERS - QUEUE UP T2 WIND (AIM FOR 60) - QUEUE UP MORE CONVERTERS (16 T2 WIND TO 6 T2 CONVERTERS) - 6 T2 CON TURRETS AT DOOR - 12 DRAGON’S FURYS AT DOOR - 4 FLAK TURRETS - 2 SHIELDS - ADD MORE DEFENCES WHERE NEEDED - MOVE INTO AFUS PRODUCTION UNTIL 50K ENERGY - START EPIC FUSIONS AND CONVERTERS


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_290.webp)


QUEUE UP 5 T1 CONSTRUCTORS AND USE THE BASE BUILDER TURRET TO SPEED UP THEIR PRODUCTION. WHEN ALL 5 ARE DONE MAKE SURE TO RESET BASE BUILDER TURRET BY SELECTING IT AND PRESSING (G) BY DEFAULT

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_293.webp)

SEND YOUR FIRST 3 CONSTRUCTORS TO HELP MAKE AND FINISH YOUR T1 WIND 64 IN TOTAL. RALLY THE LAST TWO CONSTRUCTORS BUILDING TO THE MIDDLE OF YOUR BASE

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_295.webp)

ONCE T2 LAB IS UP CONSTRUCT FIVE T2 CONSTRUCTORS AND RALLY THEM BEHIND YOUR T1 CON TURRETS 


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_298.webp)

QUEUE UP 6 T2 CONVERTERS WITH THE FIRST T2 CONSTRUCTOR RALLY THE REMAINING 4 CONSTRUCTORS TO THE SIDES SHOWN.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_300.webp)



![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_304.webp)

RECLAIM BOTH YOUR T2 AND T1 LABS FOR A REFUND IN METAL TO KICK START T2 WIND PRODUCTION AND MAINTAIN T2 CONVERTER PRODUCTION.
QUEUE UP T2 WIND PRODUCTION WITH YOUR 4 T2 CONSTRUCTORS MANUALLY SELECTING THEIR PATHING HELPS HERE MAKE SURE THEY DON’T GET STUCK OR WASTE TIME PATH FINDING.
YOU MAY NEED TO PAUSE WIND PRODUCTION OR CONVERTER PRODUCTION DEPENDING ON IF YOU NEED MORE E OR M DEPENDING ON WHAT YOUR CON TURRETS ASSIST WITH MORE THE IDEA HERE IS TO NOT WASTE ENERGY SO MAKE SURE YOU HAVE ENOUGH CONVERTERS FOR HOW MUCH E YOU’RE PRODUCING


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_309.webp)



![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_312.webp)



![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_321.webp)


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_322.webp)


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_323.webp)

SUPPLEMENT FRONT DEFENCES WITH DRAGON’S CLAWS FROM COMMANDER AND KEEP COMMANDER AT THE FRONT.
YOU MIGHT WANT TO SEND YOUR T2 CONSTRUCTORS TO START BUILDING T2 WALLS ACROSS THE FRONT NOW SO LARGER RAPTORS CAN’T GET IN BUT IT’S NOT 100% NECESSARY IF YOUR MICO AND DGUN THE LARGER RAPTORS.


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_331.webp)

EXTEND YOUR CON TURRET AND SHIELD WALL MAKING SURE ALL OF THE FRONT OF YOUR BASE HAS SHIELD COVERAGE
REINFORCE THE FRONT OF YOUR BASE WITH EITHER T2 WALLS OR MORE DRAGON’S FURYS DEPENDING WHICH YOU CAN AFFORD
BRAWLER RAPTORS CAN WALK OVER AND DESTROY T1 WALLS AND COME ONLY A FEW MINS INTO THE FIRST WAVE SO YOU EITHER NEED TO DGUN THEM FAST OR HAVE T2 WALL PROTECTION FROM THEM. 


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_341.webp)

NOW IS THE TIME TO START MAKING EPIC FUSIONS AND EPIC CONVERTERS AS WELL AS ADDING MORE DEFENCES AT THE FRONT AS NEEDED (PULSAR ARE A GOOD CHOICE)
WITH THE RIGHT SET UP YOU WON’T NEED TO ADD MORE CONVERTERS PRE EPIC FUSIONS DUE TO RAPTOR RECLAIMING. MAKE SURE YOU ARE MAKING METAL STORAGE AS YOU ARE KILLING RAPTORS AND RECLAIMING THEIR METAL. IT’S NOT IN THE GUIDE AS IT’S VERY SITUATIONAL WHEN YOU NEED METAL STORAGE DEPENDING ON AGRO LEVELS.
FROM HERE IT’S MORE OF A REACTIVE GAME SO THIS IS WHERE THE GUIDE ENDS. A FEW NOTES THOUGH ARE DON’T FORGET YOU CAN ALWAYS ADD IN T3 AIR CONS OR T3 DECOY CONS TO GIVE YOU MORE BUILD POWER AND OF COURSE ACCESS TO T3 CON TURRETS.

## Map Layout
MAP LAYOUT GUIDE
This section of the guide will provide you understanding on these key points
What an example of the positioning in the map means
What position in the map you should take relative to skill level and style of play
Understanding the role each position
All of these points that will be explained are relative to how the game plays out and how other players around you play as well as their skill levels.
MAP EXAMPLE IS BASED ON 14x14 FOR CORNER AND MIDDLE 11X12 FOR SIDES

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_371.webp)

**TLDR:**
Mid hardest to play, takes agro, big eco, high queen dps
Side for newer players, minimal agro, average eco, support
Corner carries the queen phase, large eco, highest dps, unit spam

**MIDDLE CARRY:**
A position for a more experienced player
Aims to have the second largest eco in the game
Most likely to take the most agro early thus have great metal income
Needs to invest more in defences early compared to other bases


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_383.webp)

**SIDE SUPPORT:**
Newer player position
Aims to have a stable eco and invest in defence
Takes smaller agro but deals with it easily
Focus is on defences to cover the carries LRPC>T3 UNITS


![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_390.webp)

**CORNER CARRY:**
Veteran player position
Needs to have big economy
Looking to have 60+ LRPC
T3 Unit spam to kill Queens
The corner position is the most important for getting to the Queen stage they will have the highest economy which means the most expensive defences. This role is not for newer players as it can be very macro intense to play. Knowing what to build and where to use the space efficiently is key. Playing here allows you to build the largest economy  which does mean you’re likely to have the largest amount of agro from the raptors in the late game. That said you will need to make a “chunky” front line of defences. You can do this by using the more high HP defences over the use of the more “long range” defences available to you. Having an understanding of how to efficiently eco properly is needed.

![](/guides/old-newcomers/image_397.webp)
