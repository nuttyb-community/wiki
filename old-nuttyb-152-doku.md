# **Tips for playing**

This mod is about defense gameplay, so building an efficient defense layout that scales is key to victory.

## 💕 Very important tips

🦕 Raptors prioritize eco production structures

Wind turbines, solar collectors, energy converters…

🔋 Prioritization is based on eco structure output

**Advanced Fusion (+3000E)** attracts more raptors compared to **Fusion (+1000E)**

⚠️ T2 eco attracts **4X** more raptors per resource point compared to T1

🏦 The more eco you have, the more raptors coming your way

⚖️ Balancing raptor income and your defense is key

## ✨ Other helpful tips

🌿 Metal extractors are **disabled**. Use converters

🥚 Dead raptors will drop resources

🧱 Build walls to funnel raptors

🏹 Static turrets are more efficient

📐 Map edges and corners are safer

👩‍❤️‍💋‍👩 Team up with your friends!

# **Performance considerations**

Raptor games are often played on big maps with thousands of units. Consider these pieces of advice if you experience lags.

⛰️🚫 **Enable “Disable Map Deformation” in lobby setup**

⚠️ *This won’t work with water level setting!*

Recalculating line-of-sight things when the map deforms due to explosions is quite expensive at the scale of these Raptor games.

🧱🚫 **Don't fully wall off large areas of economy buildings**.

A bit of context: Raptors usually try to target areas of strong economy
(yes they have full knowledge of where things are). If a raptor is commanded to move into an area which is unreachable, for example because it is walled off, then the pathfinder will have to search the entire pathing space to be sure that there is indeed no way to path there, and find the closest possible point that it can reach. This is quite expensive to do, and can result in large lag spikes.

💹🚫
**Performance Settings**

See the **[🛠️ Widgets](../Widgets)** page for widget performance recommendations.

 Waves of Raptors will begin spawning from their hives once the Grace period has expired, use this time to grow your resources and prepare your defenses.

 The higher the difficulty setting, the shorter the base grace period.

 The greater the evolution level, the more diverse the tentacles and waves become.

 A range of adaptations from paralyzing artillery through Kamikaze birds, LRPC to Meteor (Nuke) launchers.

 Destroying the hives reduces this evolution, however this also angers the queen. This anger does subside, but the Queen spawn timer will tick faster.

 Spawned
 Raptors will try to reach your primary economy structures. Use walls and strategic eco placement to guide them to your heaviest defenses.

 The queen will become resistant to unit attacks earlier, the higher the difficulty. A diverse attack force is advised.

 This is a Cooperative game mode. Communicate and work with your team to share resources, defensive load, roles and faction constructors, ensuring you’re working as a cohesive force.

**USEFUL TIPS**

 If using "Always Start Box", tentacles can spawn on their creep. outside the box.

If using “Initial Start Box”,
 Tentacles will only spawn on their creep, Hives will prioritize creep but can spawn outside, where there’s no radar/LoS.

 Ground
 Kamikaze Raptors are extremely fast, run in packs and can devastate defenses. If you're able to funnel them, Juno effect fields are an effective counter to them.

 Flying
 Kamikaze Raptors can explode even when shot down. Ensure you kill them far enough in front of your defenses that the inertia of their corpse does not reach your defenses, or kill above and allow them to crash land in a vacant area behind.

 Plasma
 Deflector shields are effective against all of their artillery
 (except hatchlings, unless using absorb/deflect ALL), but can easily become overwhelmed if you don't cull the herd.

 Raptor tentacles do include anti-nukes, which start spawning at 15% evo and begin stockpiling missiles. If you’re going to try nuking in the late game, you’ll have to deplete their stockpile first. This will be dozens or even hundreds of missiles depending on settings, number of players and play style.

 It can be helpful to trade Queen anger/aggression for Evolution level to delay the arrival of certain Raptor tech such as Artillery,
 Kamikazes or Meteors.

 You may wish to disable Nuke Launch notification, as it becomes irritating when more than 10 times a minute. Settings ->
 Notifications -> lower left corner

What tech arrives at what evolution level will change from time to time for balance reasons, keep yourself up to date with these to remain an effective Commander.
